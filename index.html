Welcome!

Welcome to Custom Stickers! This website is not finished, but it will be a store where you can make and purchase custom stickers for water bottles, laptops, bumpers, and other places. You can use them for personal use or for (very) small-scale promotional purposes.

Pages

To browse for pre-made stickers, click "Stickers" on the navigation bar. If you want to create stickers, click "Create".

While you shop, you can add items to your "Shopping Cart". Clearing your browser data will will clear the cart. To purchase items and manage your shopping cart, click "Your Shopping Cart".
